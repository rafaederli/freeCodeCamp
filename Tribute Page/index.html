The Enigma of Time

A journey between past, present and future

About the Book

In a world where time can be manipulated, Lara discovers an old diary that takes her on an adventure where every decision changes the timeline. Is it possible to change the past without destroying the future?

Reasons to Read

  • Engaging mystery and unexpected twists
  • Captivating and well-built characters
  • Mixture of science fiction and psychological drama
  • Reflections on the impact of our choices

Excerpt from the Book

The clock's hands turned backwards. The air grew thick, and suddenly Lara was no longer there. Time had folded in on itself.
